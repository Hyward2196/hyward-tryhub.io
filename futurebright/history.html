OUR HISTORY

FutureBright University was established in 1991 with a forward-thinking mission: to prepare students for the digital and economic challenges of a rapidly changing world. At its inception, the university launched with just two pioneering academic programs—Computer Science and Business Administration.

Housed in a modest facility with fewer than 300 students, FutureBright quickly became a hub for aspiring technologists and future business leaders. The Computer Science program focused on foundational programming, algorithms, and system design, while the Business Administration course emphasized entrepreneurship, finance, and strategic management—both crafted to meet emerging industry demands.

Over the years, FutureBright has maintained its core commitment to these disciplines, continuously updating its curriculum, expanding its faculty, and investing in state-of-the-art technology and innovation labs. Today, the legacy of these two founding programs continues to shape the university's identity and impact in the global academic community.
